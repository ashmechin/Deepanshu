Shifting Business Thoughts to Reality
 OUTSOURCING

Every year billions of dollars worth of software projects are developed in countries such as Russia, China and India. With outsourcing the software development to these countries help in bringing down the overall cost compared to developing them locally and also help in getting the specialized services in terms of technical expertise.

India emerged as a leading software service provider in the early stage with its highly skilled work force and economic conditions. In current scenario, Indian IT companies have a significant presence in global software development market and are in a state to give tough competition to any international service providers with their technical skills and management capabilities.

With the vast improvement in communication technology, like virtual offices, VOIP services, various options to live meetings have increase the feasibility of offshore software development as compared to few years ago. Outsourcing also helps in giving more concentration to core business instead of spending the resources on developing the system for businesses.

The Benefits of Outsourcing

Flexibility

Offshore Software Development gives an option to get the different skill sets on one location. For short duration and key projects, instead of hiring new resources, one can offshore the requirements and can get the things done quickly. This flexibility help in saving the efforts in recruitment, legal formalities and other resources required for employees.

Economical Benefits

Whether a small organization, big corporate or any individual, the remuneration of offshore development can be immense. As the cost of offshore development is a fraction of the actual cost, It allows clients to save a lot in terms of investment. All the expenses needed for hiring a resource, compliance to legal formalities etc will get vanished due to virtual teams.

Independent Analysis

Offshore development helps in saving resources which are required for conducting independent analysis of the product before going to market and competitors. Offshore companies not only provide services in development activities but do the evaluation of competitor’s products, which help in making the new product robust in all aspects.

Technology Catalyst

For completing any development, a number of different technologies are required and offshore services comes handy in this scenario as they contain a set of diverse skill sets due to different kind of exposures from diverse set of customers requirements.

Business Innovation

Offshore development help in improving the project management, technical skills and technology variation due to diverse exposure and help in spending efforts for more innovation in the respective business.
